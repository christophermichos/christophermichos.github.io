Blog post four, Cannabis

Posted by chris on sunday

This is my fourth blog and I am talking about cannabis. Cannabis is a massive industry and will continue to grow in popularity for recreational purposes as well as a replacement to many traditional pharmaceutical medications. The management of the Cannabis supply chain requires proper materials managment from the right storage conditions to using a proper supply calculations to avoid high inventory costs and deterioration of the product.
